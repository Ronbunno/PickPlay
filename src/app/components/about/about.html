My Mission

Why I built PickPlay.

Why did I build PickPlay? Because I wanted to grow as a developer and improve my skills in programming—especially with Angular and modern tools like Tailwind CSS and TypeScript. But more than that, I wanted to create something meaningful for gamers like me—a place to discover new games, share excitement, and enjoy the gaming world together.
